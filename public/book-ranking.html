The Popular Science Books According to Muniyandi

My Top 3 Science Books of All Time

1. A Short History of Nearly Everything

It connects the dots between different scientific disciplines (geology, physics, biology) to give you a complete picture of the Earth's history.

2. Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind

It offers a unique perspective on evolutionary biology,
explaining not just how we evolved,
but why we behave the way we do today.

3. Cosmos

For the sheer beauty of the writing and to gain a sense of perspective regarding humanity's place in the universe.
